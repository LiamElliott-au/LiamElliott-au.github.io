Welcome

February 22, 2018 PERSONAL
  • about

It’s time to stop procrastinating… For many years now I’ve spoken on many occasions with colleagues about the importance of keeping a blog, even tonight I interviewed Thiago Passos for SSW TV on his last year or so (still waiting for it to be published), and how he has been working on building his profile, both as a blogger and as a speaker. In closing the interview, I promised Thiago I’d write my first post, and now I’m out of excuses. Before I knew it, he had forwarded me a link to his post for setting up a ghost blog on Azure, 5 mins later, and here I am.

2018 challenges

Toward the end of 2017, I made 2 personal commitments to myself:

  1. To read 12 books; I’ve never been a reader so this will be tougher than it sounds
  2. To write 12 blog posts; It’s almost March, so I’m already behind

What to expect

I’m not quite sure at this stage, and I’m sure the nature of the posts will evolve as will my maturity in writing them. On these pages, I hope to cover various topics on my hobbies and passions. From Enterprise Software, to the books I am reading and possibly even my photography.
